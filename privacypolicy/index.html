Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 06/20/25

The Dunklin R-V Performing Arts website, operated by Andrew Brock, Director of Choirs and Theater, is committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of all visitors.

Purpose of This Website

This website serves as the official hub for all choir, theater, and band-related activities at Dunklin R-V School District. It provides updates, event information, downloadable forms, ticket sales, and contact information for faculty.

Personal Information

We do not directly collect personal information through this website. However, certain features or services may involve third-party platforms that collect user information.

Ticket Purchases

All ticket sales are processed through Eventbrite. Eventbrite may collect personal details such as name, email address, and payment information. Please refer to Eventbrite's own privacy policy for more details.

Forms and Submissions

The site may include downloadable or Google Forms used by students and parents. Any information submitted via those forms is handled according to the terms and privacy policies of the respective platforms.

Contact Options

Visitors may use the Team directory or quick contact links to reach out to faculty members. Any information submitted via email or other means is voluntarily provided by the user.

Embedded Social Media

This website includes embedded social media feeds and links from:

  • Facebook (Choir and Theater)
  • Instagram (Theater)
  • TikTok (Theater)

These platforms may collect data through cookies or other technologies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies to understand how your information is used.
